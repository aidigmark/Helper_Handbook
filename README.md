## Quick Start: 5 Ways to Help Today

1. **5 minutes**: Text a friend going through a hard time
2. **15 minutes**: Sign up for [Be My Eyes](https://bemyeyes.com) to help blind users
3. **30 minutes**: Donate blood at your local blood bank
4. **1 hour**: Sort food at a local food bank
5. **Ongoing**: Set up a monthly $5 donation to a [GiveWell top charity](https://givewell.org)

## The Helper's Mindset

Before diving in, remember:

> "The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now."

You don't need to be perfect. You don't need to solve everything. You just need to start, stay consistent, and keep learning. Every person who shifts from passive concern to active helping makes the world measurably better.

Let's begin.
