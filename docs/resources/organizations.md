## How to Evaluate Organizations Yourself

If an organization isn't listed here, research them:

1. **Check evaluators**: GiveWell, Charity Navigator, GuideStar, BBB Wise Giving Alliance
2. **Read financials**: Annual reports, Form 990 (for US nonprofits)
3. **Look for evidence**: Do they measure and report outcomes?
4. **Search for criticism**: What do detractors say?
5. **Talk to people**: Beneficiaries, volunteers, former staff
