# Donation Tracker Template

Track your charitable giving for impact analysis, tax purposes, and personal reflection.

---

## How to Use This Template

1. Set your annual giving budget
2. Decide on allocation strategy (see Chapter 4)
3. Log each donation when you make it
4. Keep receipts/confirmation emails
5. Review annually to assess and adjust

## Tax Documentation Checklist

For US taxpayers itemizing deductions:

☐ Gathered all donation receipts over $250
☐ Recorded organization names, dates, and amounts
☐ Noted any non-cash donations with fair market value
☐ Checked that all organizations are 501(c)(3) qualified
☐ Filed receipts with tax documents
☐ Considered donor-advised fund for future tax efficiency

**Note:** Donations over $250 require written acknowledgment from the charity. Keep these records for at least 3 years.
